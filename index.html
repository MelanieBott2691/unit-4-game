ColorCollector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four colors below. By clicking on a color you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number.

You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each color is hidden from you until you click on it.

The game will change the values of the colors each time the game starts.
